Explore Yunnan

Yunnan is the most southwestern province in China, with the Tropic of Cancer running through its southern part. It is situated in a mountainous area, with high elevations in the northwest and low elevations in the southeast. It has a generally mild climate with pleasant and fair weather because of the province's location on south-facing mountain slopes. Such geographical and climatic features have created a natural landscape unique to Yunnan.

Experience Yunnan

With its snow-capped peaks, rice terraces, forests, and lakes and gorges, China's south western province of Yunnan offers one of the most beautiful and diverse landscapes in the country. Known for its large number of ethnic minorities (more than 25), its dotted with traditional tea houses, stunning pagodas, and many museums and temples, leaving a lasting impression on all those who come to experience it.
